The Best Movies According to Angela

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

  1. The Lord of the Rings

    2. My all-time favorite fantasy series. I love the deep relationships between the characters, their values, and the powerful storytelling.

  2. Far and Away

    3. A romantic and inspiring film that shows how passion, dreams, and determination can bring people together.

  3. Taken

    4. A powerful and emotional action movie that shows how far a father is willing to go to protect his daughter. A true example of unconditional love.
